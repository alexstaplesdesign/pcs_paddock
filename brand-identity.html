01

Who We Are

More Than
a Restaurant

PC's Paddock is a family-owned American restaurant nestled inside a lovingly renovated 1840s barn in Poughkeepsie, New York. Since opening in 2007, we've been the kind of place where neighbors linger, birthdays are celebrated, and regulars are known by name. Our menu centers on upscale comfort food — smoked low-and-slow on our onsite smoker, finished with herbs pulled fresh from our garden. We offer relaxed dine-in, seasonal outdoor seating, takeout, catering, and delivery. We're not just a meal — we're a gathering place.

02

Logo System

The Mark Family

Shield Mark — Primary

Primary logo for all official applications.
Use on dark or textured backgrounds.

Round Badge — Secondary

Social media, circular crops, stamps.
Use on cream or light backgrounds.

Alt Mark — Horizontal

Website headers, letterheads, menus.
Use when wide format is needed.

Logo Usage Rules

DO
Maintain minimum size of 120px (shield mark)
Preserve clear space equal to the height of the "P"
Scale proportionally, always locking aspect ratio
Use only on approved backgrounds (dark green, cream, off-white)
NEVER
Stretch, squish, or distort the logo in any direction
Recolor outside the approved brand palette
Add drop shadows, glows, or outline effects
Place on busy, clashing, or low-contrast backgrounds

Color Usage Guidelines

Accessibility First

All text/background pairs must meet WCAG AA contrast (4.5:1 minimum). Never use cream text on cream or gold text on off-white.

Dominant + Accent Rule

Hunter Green dominates. Gold accents no more than 20% of any composition. Sage used for large background areas only.

03

Color Pairings

Approved Combinations

Hunter Green + Cream

Primary pairing. Nav, headers, hero sections.

Off-White + Charcoal

Body content. Best for long-form reading.

Hunter Green + Gold

CTA buttons on dark backgrounds.

Light Sage + Charcoal

Feature cards, menu sections.

Dark Green + Cream

Footer, deep overlays, cover pages.

Cream + Gold

Dividers, secondary buttons on light backgrounds.

Application Notes

Digital

Website hero: Hunter Green background with Cream heading, Gold CTA button. Navigation: Hunter Green bar, Lato Bold nav items.

Print

Menus: Off-White background, Charcoal body text, Hunter Green section headers, Gold rule dividers. Covers: Dark Green.

Signage

Exterior: Dark Green background with Cream and Gold lettering. Chalkboards: Charcoal surface with Cream chalk-effect type.

Accessibility & Contrast

Hunter Green on Cream: 9.2:1 — exceeds AAA

Cream on Dark Green: 10.4:1 — exceeds AAA

Charcoal on Off-White: 13.8:1 — exceeds AAA

Gold on Dark Green: 4.8:1 — passes AA
