Family-Owned · Since 2007

A Barn Built for Good Times

Nestled on Titusville Road in Poughkeepsie, PC's Paddock has been welcoming Hudson Valley locals and visitors since 2007 — right inside a lovingly renovated 1840s barn. From the weathered wood beams overhead to the warmth of the bar, every corner tells a story.

"Upscale comfort food, rustic charm, and seasonal garden seating — all under one roof."

Our onsite smoker runs all week, slow-cooking signature dishes like our Award-Winning Wings and Smoked Prime Rib to tender perfection. Fresh herbs come straight from our own garden out back, finding their way into everything from craft cocktails to house dressings.

Whether you're sliding into the pub for happy hour or settling in for a full dinner with family, we set the table the same way every time — with heart.
